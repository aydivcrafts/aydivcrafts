Workshops


Workshops are broadly categorized into corporate workshops and kids’ workshops. Kids’ workshops generally happen around vacations school breaks and extends for a couple of days to weeks. One day workshops and hourly workshops are also conducted that cover one or two product types at the max. Depending on the number of enquiries, the date and venue gets decided. Our workshops provide all the materials required and also give you a certificate at the end of the session, along with pointers to find the materials online and offline. Our corporate workshops are stress-busting sessions providing a fun filled crafty time to all the participants.

All workshops and sessions are conducted under our banner “AirNCraft”. Check out the website (www.airncraft.com/offering/workshops) for updates on our upcoming workshops.
