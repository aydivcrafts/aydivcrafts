About Us

Welcome to Aydiv Crafts! We make exclusively handmade customized gifting solutions for all occasions! Our range is built around eco-friendly materials like paper, cardstock and wood, which do not harm the environment. At our boutique, we could do a completely custom-made gift and / or also create a replica of an item of your choice. We ensure that we deliver the right product with great detail.

You can request for an exact replica of one our existing products or even ask for changes in the same! Even more, you can place an order for a completely new product which is still a basic idea in your mind and we will make sure it is made just like you imagined! Have a look at our products and give us an opportunity to make the product of your choice that fits just right in your budget! The products can be shipped to any place in the world, if you are ready to bear the shipping cost!

Vidya Ravichandran ( Founder and Creative Head )


Mrs. Vidya, the founder and owner of Aydiv Crafts, is an Engineering Graduate from BITS, Pilani and an ex-software engineer. Taking a break from her job, she and her husband together founded their technology startup, LadderMinds at Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. Stepping into the world of entrepreneurship as the Co-Founder and CEO of LadderMinds, also paved the way to create Aydiv Crafts, a boutique that makes customized and personalized gifts for its customers.


She is also the Operations Director at Acodeidea Technologies Private Limited (www.acodeidea.com), another home startup providing consulting services to an overseas client with an employee count of fourteen people at present. She holds partnership at LadderMinds Corporate Ventures (www.ladderminds.com) which manages various brands under its banner such as AirNCraft (www.airncraft.com) (An evolutionary platform offering activities, workshops and contests for the creative and geeky minds in crafts and aero sciences) , Active Dabba (www.activedabba.com) (An exclusive online store for creative activity boxes involving themes like Crafts, Aeromodelling and so on) , NotesMinute (www.notesminute.com) (Making remembering notes easier by providing pointers in the form of headings and small tips about the topic) , with many more service brands in line. Apart from managing the above startups, she also offers advisory services to the aeronautical startup Wethe5pilots (www.wethe5pilots.com), which is co-founded by her husband.
